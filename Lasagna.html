Description

This is a complete guide to your first selfmade lasagna. Just take some ingredients and let's start!

Ingredients:

  • 200gr. of flour
  • 200ml of nice milk
  • 500gr of minced beef
  • 20 tomatoes

Just take all of the ingredients and mix them. Put them in a pan and fry them until golden brown. After 20 minutes built your lasagna, layer by layer. Add some butter for a better taste. Let it sit for 2 minutes and then put it into the oven. Now you've made your first own lasagna! Congratulation!

Things you need now:

  1. Dishes
  2. Coke
  3. Barkeeper

Now enjoy your Time!
